The mark — “The First Line”

A home drawn in a single unbroken stroke — except it isn't finished. The right wall is a pencil, point down, one line from done; the gap in the floor is the line not yet drawn. Temporary accommodation is a house that's never quite finished: booked for the week, gone on Friday. At small sizes the pencil reads simply as a tapered stroke, so the mark stays clean; at scale, the idea appears.

Rules

  • Clearspace: one door-width (¼ of mark height) on all sides.
  • Minimum size: 16 px digital, 8 mm print, 20 mm embroidery.
  • Wordmark is always lowercase; never re-set it in another weight or typeface.
  • Never fill the mark, outline the wordmark, rotate, or add effects.
  • Don't separate the pencil from the house — the gap in the floor stays.
  • UI and body typeface: Inter. Headlines Inter Bold, tracked −2%.
